# KamPerformance Design System

## Brand Direction

KamPerformance is an evidence-based movement education brand.

Primary focus:
Education first. Services second.

## Core Message

Understand Movement. Move Better.

## Brand Signature

Understanding movement is the first step toward moving better.

## Design Principles

1. White space is intentional.
2. Every section has one purpose.
3. Typography creates hierarchy.
4. Consistency creates trust.
5. Animation supports interaction.

## Color Palette
