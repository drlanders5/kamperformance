EVIDENCE FIRST

Research before trends.

KamPerformance is built on evidence-based education, clinical reasoning, biomechanics, and practical movement science.

OUR APPROACH

Clear education starts with better reasoning.

Movement advice should not be based on fear, trends, or one-size-fits-all rules. KamPerformance focuses on helping people understand the reasoning behind movement recommendations.

Evidence-Based

Education is guided by research, biomechanics, exercise science, pain science, and clinical reasoning.

Context Matters

Pain, mobility, and performance are influenced by the person, the task, the environment, and the goal.

No Fear-Based Education

The goal is to reduce confusion and fear, not create more worry around normal movement.

WHAT THIS MEANS

Education before intervention.

Before choosing an exercise, stretch, mobility drill, or training plan, it helps to understand the problem being addressed and the reason behind the recommendation.
