FAQ

Common questions.

Is KamPerformance physical therapy?

KamPerformance provides movement education and general educational resources. It is not a replacement for individualized medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Can I request a topic?

Yes. Topic requests related to pain, mobility, performance, exercise, or movement education are welcome.

Will training services be available?

Training and coaching options are planned for the future. Until scheduling is finalized, Linktree and social media are the best ways to follow updates.
