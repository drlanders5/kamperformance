FAQ

Common questions.

Is KamPerformance physical therapy?

KamPerformance provides movement education and general educational resources. It is not a replacement for individualized medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Can I request a topic?

Yes. If there is a movement, pain, mobility, or performance topic you want explained, you can send a request by email.

Do you offer collaborations?

Collaboration requests related to education, movement content, health, performance, or professional opportunities are welcome.

Where should I start?

The Movement Library is the best starting point for learning about pain, mobility, performance, and exercise concepts.
