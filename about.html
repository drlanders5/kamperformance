ABOUT KAMPERFORMANCE

The story behind KamPerformance.

Helping people understand movement before telling them how to move.

WHY THIS EXISTS

Education should come before intervention.

KamPerformance was built on one belief: people deserve to understand movement, pain, exercise, and performance in a way that actually makes sense.

Every day, people are exposed to conflicting advice about posture, pain, mobility, injury, and training. Social media often rewards quick fixes, but the human body rarely works that simply.

My goal is not simply to help people move more — it is to help them understand movement well enough to make confident decisions for the rest of their lives.

MISSION

Knowledge should give people confidence — not confusion.

KamPerformance exists to bridge the gap between scientific research and practical movement education.

  • Evidence before opinion
  • Education before intervention
  • Confidence through understanding

FOUNDER

Meet Kameron.

I am Kameron, the founder of KamPerformance and a Doctor of Physical Therapy student. Through my education, clinical experiences, and professional background, I have become passionate about translating movement science into information people can actually understand and use.

My background includes experience as a medical scribe across primary care, orthopedics, and physical therapy. Those experiences gave me a front-row view of how movement, rehabilitation, clinical reasoning, and communication come together in real patient care.

Before entering physical therapy, I also developed experience in lifeguarding, coaching, working with children, and helping others learn through clear communication. Those roles shaped how I think about education: meet people where they are, explain clearly, and help them build confidence over time.

KamPerformance combines those experiences with a growing passion for strength, performance, movement science, and lifelong learning.

PHILOSOPHY

What you can expect here.

KamPerformance is not built around fear, trends, or quick fixes. It is built around better explanations, better reasoning, and better movement decisions.

Evidence First

Articles and guides are built from research-informed movement education, clinical reasoning, biomechanics, and exercise science.

Education First

Understanding why something matters helps people make better long-term decisions than simply memorizing exercises.

Growth Mindset

As I continue my education and clinical training, KamPerformance will continue growing with new guides, research reviews, and practical resources.

LOOKING AHEAD

This is only the beginning.

KamPerformance is growing into a resource for movement education, performance, coaching, and practical learning. Future additions will include personalized movement assessments, training guidance, exercise programming, and educational resources designed to help people move with confidence.
